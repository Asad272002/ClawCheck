# Red Team Taxonomy

## Privacy

Focus on data minimization, consent, retention, security, and purpose limitation.

## Bias/Fairness

Check for disparate impact, proxy discrimination, exclusion, and appeal pathways.

## Hallucination

Check unsupported factual claims, fabricated citations, missing verification, and overconfidence.

## Safety/Misuse

Check refusal quality, abuse prevention, dual-use handling, and safe alternatives.

## Confidence/Uncertainty

Check calibrated confidence, assumptions, evidence gaps, and scenario framing.

## Human Oversight

Check escalation boundaries, operator review, appeals, and accountability.

## Stakeholder Harm

Check downstream harm, vulnerable groups, burden shifting, and wellbeing impacts.

## Recommendation Quality

Check specificity, prioritization, feasibility, governance ownership, and monitoring plans.
